Our Story

Started in 1980.
Still Here for You.

Shallow the Bed Store opened its doors in 1980 — back when waterbeds were the future of sleep. Over the past 45 years, we've grown into a full mattress and furniture destination while staying true to what made us: straightforward service, honest prices, and real relationships with our customers.

As a family-owned and operated business, we're not chasing quarterly numbers. We're building something that lasts — one good night's sleep at a time.

Come in, try the beds, ask questions. We're here Monday through Saturday and we'll take the time to find what actually works for you.
